# Product Roadmap

## Stage 1 — Foundation (0-2 Months)
- Stabilize continuous vital monitoring and wearable comfort.
- Deliver baseline gait, posture, and activity telemetry.
- Launch real-time app dashboard with BLE connectivity.

## Stage 2 — Safety Intelligence (2-4 Months)
- Deliver AI fall-risk prediction and instability detection.
- Deliver preventive haptics and caregiver fall-risk notifications.
- Deliver emergency response automation with SOS and GPS sharing.

## Stage 3 — Behavioral + Wellness Intelligence (4-6 Months)
- Add routine analysis, cognitive indicators, and social isolation detection.
- Add medication/hydration/activity reminders and adherence tracking.
- Add sleep quality and long-term mobility trend analytics.

## Stage 4 — Predictive Healthcare Platform (6+ Months)
- Deliver personalized risk models and anomaly detection at scale.
- Deliver health report generation and family/caregiver portal maturity.
- Deliver continuous health assessment with preventive recommendations.

## Persistent Product Outcomes
- Independent living support for elderly users.
- Real-time caregiver visibility and intervention support.
- Secure, cloud-backed, longitudinal health intelligence.
