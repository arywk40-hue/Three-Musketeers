ElderCare Guardian — Last updated June 2026

1. Service Description

ElderCare Guardian is a wellness-monitoring companion application that collects and displays heart rate, SpO₂, temperature, movement, and other biometric data from a paired Bluetooth Low Energy wearable device. The app is designed to assist caregivers in monitoring the well-being of elderly individuals.

2. Not a Medical Device

ElderCare Guardian is not a certified medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. All biometric data presented is for informational and wellness-tracking purposes only. You should not rely on the app for medical decision-making. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical advice.

3. No Emergency Services

The app does not connect to emergency services (police, ambulance, or fire departments). Caregiver alerts are sent via SMS or push notification to a user-configured phone number. Do not use the app as a sole means of emergency communication.

4. Data Privacy

All health data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to third parties unless you explicitly enable cloud backup. You may export or delete your data at any time via the app settings. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

5. User Responsibility
